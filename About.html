Paula Gonzalez is a recent graduate of MIT, where she received a B.S. in Civil and Environmental Engineering with a focus in urban studies and policy. She is fascinated by the intersection of urban development and the environment, and has the hopes of investigating this interest within the context of the developing world.

Paula is currently working as a strategic analyst at the Air Liquide i-Lab in Paris, France, exploring the connections between urban trends, air quality, and health.

Beginning in Autumn 2015, Paula will begin a two-year fellowship with Venture for America, and she is eager to participate in the revitalization of an American city from the ground up. Check out her current and past work here!
