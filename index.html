Hello, world!

My name is Paula Gonzalez and I am a graduating senior in the
Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at MIT.

As an engineering major with a growing interest in urban planning and policy, I am interested in how technology intersects with the urban lifestyle, specifically in how cities can harness the vast amounts of data created by its inhabitants. With new technologies in sensing and actuating, cities have more and more tools at their disposal with which they can improve the lives of their citizens.

Check out the other pages on this site to see more of what I've been working on while at MIT!
